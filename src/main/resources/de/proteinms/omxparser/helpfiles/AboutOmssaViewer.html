About OMSSA Viewer

OMMSA Viewer was developed by Harald Barsnes when a PhD student at University of Bergen, Norway, and under the guidance of Prof. Dr. Lennart Martens (UGent / VIB).

It is a simple, lightweight viewer for OMSSA OMX files and is a part of the OMSSA Parser project.

Project home page: http://compomics.github.io/projects/omssa-parser.html.

For a more advanced viewer of OMSSA search results we recommend PeptideShaker.
